Courses Offered

The B. Tech in Computer Science and Engineering aims to develop graduates that are not only well versed with traditional computing approaches but are also experienced in modern tools, technologies, and diverse applications.
The program has a comprehensive set of fundamental courses in computer science and many electives. This enables the students to build a program most suitable for them.
The program will prepare students to work in the IT industry as well as in the research areas like computer vision, machine learning, image processing, pattern recognition, virtual/augmented reality, etc.
The program will also provide current research trends for students, who want to pursue higher studies, to take up higher studies in CS.

Our Facilities

GEU campus is not just a backdrop; but it is the foregrounds of your future. It provides a diverse range of cultural, recreational and social activities. With so many choices, students can learn new skills or sports; discover talents they never knew they had and make friendships that will create memories for a lifetime. The beautifully designed Graphic Era Deemed to be University campus has stimulating aesthetics and is perpetually in motion, with plethora of activities taking place, there is never a dull moment. We maintain an open and inclusive environment that nurtures the growth and development of the student community. Here you will find compelling opportunities in the arts, health and wellness, community volunteerism and more. We also offer a variety of student services, programs and other resources to help you reach your personal and educational goals.

GEU has umpteen numbers of exciting experiences available to enrich your campus life. It is entertaining, and incredibly rewarding with vastly unique activities that vary from academic activities, extracurricular activities and performing arts to live performances by the celebrity artists. There are sunny spots to offer a place to relax and socialize with fellow students, fitness centres and sports centre for outdoor activities. There will be many magical moments, during the annual Grafest, when the whole university pulsates with music and dances. We assure you that your stay with us will be an experience worth cherishing.

The sports committee

The Sports committee believes in developing the overall personality of students. It organizes inter and intra-college level football, cricket, basketball tournaments. There are also facilities for all indoor games including Table Tennis, Badminton, Billiards, Carom and Chess etc. The sport week is organized in each session where students participate with full enthusiasm.

Library

The University has established a spacious Central Library (Santosh Anand Library) located in the main campus. The University subscribes to 127 Journals/Periodicals and 5242+ E-Journals and is an institutional member of Access Millions of Networked Library Resources through DELNET. The library supports the NPTEL platform. Online Journals and Scopus are available besides Anti-Plagiarism software Turnitin.

Food Court

Variety is the spice of life - this is what our cafeterias reflect. Our canteens offers vegetarian delicacies and caters to the diverse palates, and for the health freaks, we have a dedicated juice counter providing fresh fruit juices. Delicious food is prepared according to strict standards of hygiene.

Guest House

A guest house comprising 12 AC and Non-AC rooms is available within the campus to the guardians on actual availability basis on nominal charges.

E-Content Development Facility

A place to let your imagination run wild and creativity flow with ease, this is an incubation for brainstorming, design and execution. Here, one can capture sessions of lecturers for digital learning, record multiple means of media (audio/visual), and create the best of content for their audience and portfolio.

Hostels

Residential Living provides a safe environment in innumerable residence halls, dining service with nutritious and sustainable meals, cultivates community feeling and enhances your educational experience on campus. Every effort is made that the hostellers get a homely ambience by providing them a home away from home.
